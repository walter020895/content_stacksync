## Research Template (Per Profile)

For each person, pull 5-10 top-performing posts and capture:

```
POST: [Copy or screenshot]
ESTIMATED ENGAGEMENT: [Likes/Comments]

1. HOOK TYPE: [Fortune Teller / Experimenter / Teacher / Magician / Investigator / Contrarian]
2. TOPIC: What is it about?
3. ANGLE: What's their unique spin?
4. STRUCTURE: How does the post flow?
5. WHAT WORKS: Why did this perform?
```
