Electronic Data Interchange(EDI). Purchase orders, invoices, shipping notices, inventory updates. It's been the B2B standard for decades.

The problem:
- Onboarding one trading partner takes 4-8 weeks
- Troubleshooting takes days. Visibility is close to zero
- Every partner uses different formats. X12, EDIFACT, custom
- Mapping logic is brittle. One schema change breaks the flow
- Most companies depend on consultants who profit from the complexity

The result: companies avoid adding new partners because the integration cost is too high. Growth gets blocked by plumbing.
