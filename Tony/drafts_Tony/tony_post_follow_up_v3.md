Early in my career, I chased every deal.

"Just following up!"
"Did you get a chance to look?"
"Circling back on this..."

Ten emails. No response. Deal dies anyway.

Took me years to learn the fix.

Now I set the timeline on the first call.
We agree together: "This date, we meet again."

If they reschedule? I ask one question:

"What's changed?"

Not aggressive. Not passive.
Just clear.

That question surfaces what 10 follow-ups never will.

Real objection? I can handle it.
Deal dead? I move on.
Still interested? Now we have a real conversation.

Same logic applies to your data.
