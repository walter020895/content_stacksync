I don't call prospects asking "Have you looked at it yet?"

That's chasing.
And chasing never closes.

What I do instead:

I set a timeline on the call. We both agree: "On this date, we'll meet again."

Now if they don't show or want to reschedule, I can ask one simple question:

"What's changed?"

That question does more work than ten follow-up emails.

It respects their time.
It respects mine.
And it reveals whether this deal is real or dead.

Same principle applies to your data.
