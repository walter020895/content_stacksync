20 years. Hundreds of deals. One pattern:

The reps who chase, lose.

"Just checking in!"
"Wanted to bump this up!"
"Any update?"

That's not follow-up. That's hope.

What works:

Set a timeline on the call.
Both sides agree: "On this date, we reconnect."

If they reschedule or don't show?

One question: "What's changed?"

That question does more than your entire email sequence.

It surfaces the real objection.
It shows mutual respect.
It separates live deals from dead ones.

Same with your data.
