Chasing kills deals.

I don't call prospects asking "Are you ready yet?"

I set a timeline on the call.
We both agree: "On this date, we meet again."

If they ghost or reschedule?

One question: "What's changed?"

That's it.

That question reveals what's real.
That question respects everyone's time.
That question closes.
