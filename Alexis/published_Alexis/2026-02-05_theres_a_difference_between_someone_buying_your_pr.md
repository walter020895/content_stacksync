There's a difference between someone buying your product and someone showing the room how they use it.

We hosted The SF NetSuite Series: From Silos to AI-Driven Ops.
45 NetSuite practitioners in one room. Good energy.

Fellow, Orderful, and Origami Precision on stage with us.

And then Arvind Jeyakumar from Fellow (a $100M+ specialty coffee company)
stood up and showed the room how they use Stacksync.

Their actual workflow. How they sync their NetSuite data with Stacksync in production.

That moment hit me.

I work with customers every day. But this was different.

This was someone choosing to stand in front of a room full of strangers and say: we use this. Let me show you why.

You can raise money. You can ship features. You can grow the team.

But the moment a customer talks about your product and shows it with pride, not because you asked, but because they want to?

That's something you can't build. You can only earn it.
