    Stacksync ICP — Search Terms & Tag Map

This document groups real search terms and language patterns used by Stacksync’s ICP:
Data / RevOps / Platform teams dealing with **real-time, bidirectional, operational sync**
between CRMs, ERPs, and databases — without brittle custom code.
